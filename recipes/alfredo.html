Fettuccine Alfredo

Description

This recipe is used to make Fettuccine Alfredo. This dish is typically prepared for dinner.

This meal takes around 30 minutes to prepare.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. fettuccine
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 4 oz. grated parmesan
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Heat saucepan to medium heat.
  2. Melt butter.
  3. Add pasta, cream, parmesan, salt, and pepper.
  4. Toss over low heat until well coated.
