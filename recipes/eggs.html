Deviled Eggs

Description

This recipe is for deviled eggs. This dish is typically served as an appetizer or as an horderve

This recipe takes 20 minutes. This recipe can serve up to 4 people.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Mayonnaise
  • Vinegar
  • Mustard
  • Salt
  • Paprika

Steps

  1. Hard boil eggs.
  2. Place eggs in fridge to cool.
  3. Peel eggs and cut in half.
  4. Add egg yolks, mayonnaise, mustard, vinegar, salt in a bowl and blend until smooth.
  5. Arrange egg whites on a dish.
  6. Place egg yolk mixture in pastry bag or ziploc bag.
  7. Fill each egg half with mixture.
  8. Top with paprika.
