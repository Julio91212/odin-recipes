Tostadas

Description

This recipe is used to make chicken tostadas. This dish is typically prepared for dinner.

A single serving typically consists of two tostadas.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Mustard
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Can of Peas and Carrots
  • Mayonnaise
  • Tostadas
  • Hot Sauce (e.g. Tapatio, Tabasco)

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken using mustard, salt, and pepper.
  2. Cook chicken thoroughly in pan.
  3. Let chicken cool for 10 minutes.
  4. Shred chicken using mixer or by hand.
  5. Add mayonnaise and peas and carrots to chicken and mix thoroughly.
  6. Let chicken cool in fridge for 15 minutes.
  7. Serve chicken on tostada with hot sauce.
